FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

The fastest answers to the most common questions about the ALDI Blind Boxes Giveaway.

What is the ALDI Blind Box?
The ALDI Blind Box is a free giveaway where shoppers can claim a surprise box filled with ALDI fan-favorite products, standout staples, and fresh picks from across every aisle. It's a limited-time promotion running June 22-25.
How do I claim a free ALDI Blind Box?
Follow @aldiusa on Instagram for daily theme reveals, visit the ALDI Blind Box website beginning June 22 through June 25 at 12:00 p.m. ET daily, select that day's box, enter your shipping information, and receive your free box delivered to your door.
How many types of blind boxes are there?
There are four themed boxes: Snack Blind Box (a craveable mix for grazing), Fiber Blind Box (gut-friendly staples), Protein Blind Box (fuel and fullness), and Mystery Blind Box (a secret surprise mix).
When can I claim my ALDI Blind Box?
The giveaway runs from June 22 through June 25. A new box theme drops each day at 12:00 p.m. ET. Boxes are available on a first-come, first-served basis while supplies last.
Is there a purchase required?
No. The ALDI Blind Box is completely free. There is no purchase necessary. You only need to select your box and provide shipping information to receive it at your door.
Can I find these products in stores?
While the ALDI Blind Box experience is only available for a limited time, shoppers can find the featured products and new discoveries every week in stores nationwide and on the ALDI website.
